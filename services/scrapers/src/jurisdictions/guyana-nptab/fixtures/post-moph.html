IFB — Supply of Essential Pharmaceuticals for GPHC — MOPH/2026/GY/031

April 15, 2026

The Ministry of Public Health invites sealed bids from eligible and qualified bidders for the supply of essential pharmaceuticals to the Georgetown Public Hospital Corporation. Estimated contract value is G$ 185,400,000.

Bidding documents may be collected from the NPTAB office, Main Street, Georgetown, between 09:00 and 15:00 hrs weekdays.

Bids must be submitted on 20-May-2026 at 09:00 hrs at the office of the National Procurement and Tender Administration Board. Late bids will be returned unopened.

Tender reference: MOPH/2026/GY/031.
