## Lifecycle note

This plan was drafted on 2026-04-30 for the first 30 days of
specialty crude operations. **It supersedes itself every 30 days.**
At the end of each period, a retrospective and a fresh plan get
written. Do not read this document 6 months from now and try to
execute it as if it were current.

The strategic backbone in `docs/specialty-crude-strategy.md` (in
vex, pointer here in procur) is what's durable. The 30-day plan is
a tactical snapshot.

  buildout (existing tools: web search, entity_news_events,
  analyze_supplier)
- Grade-fit analysis for the introductory call preparation
- Empirical pricing data for the discount market intelligence
  workstream that begins in Month 2-3

## What procur does NOT provide

- No compliance-route evaluation (vex)
- No transaction templates (vex)
- No deal-level execution (vex)
- No tracking of specialty crude deal pipeline (vex tenant-private)
